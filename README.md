# 📊 Power BI Retail Sales Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This Power BI Retail Sales Dashboard transforms raw retail sales data into actionable business insights. The dashboard helps businesses analyze sales performance, customer behavior, product demand, and revenue distribution across different countries.

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Analyze overall sales performance
- Identify best-selling products
- Understand revenue contribution by country
- Study customer purchasing behavior
- Support data-driven business decisions

## 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

| KPI | Value |
|------|--------|
| Total Sales | 8.91M |
| Total Quantity Sold | 5M |
| Total Orders | 397K |
| Total Customers | 397K |

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 1. Monthly Sales Trend
- Analyze sales performance over time
- Identify seasonal trends and growth patterns

### 2. Sales by Country
- Compare sales performance across countries
- Identify top-performing markets

### 3. Sales by Product
- Discover best-selling products
- Measure product contribution to revenue

### 4. Quantity by Product
- Understand product demand
- Support inventory planning

### 5. Revenue by Country
- Evaluate country-wise revenue contribution
- Identify high-value markets

## 🔍 Key Insights

- 🇬🇧 United Kingdom generates the highest sales revenue.
- 📦 A small group of products contributes significantly to total revenue.
- 📈 Sales increase toward the end of the year.
- 🌍 Some countries generate high revenue despite lower order volumes.

## 💡 Business Recommendations

### Focus Marketing
Allocate more marketing budget to top-selling products.

### Customer Retention
Develop retention strategies for high-value customers.

### Market Expansion
Expand into countries showing potential demand.

### Inventory Optimization
Improve stock management for high-demand products.
